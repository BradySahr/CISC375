Postseason

ALCS Game 1

October 8, 1991

The Twins 1991 playoff run starts with a win over the Blue Jays at home. The Twins took an early 5-0 lead and hang on to win 5-4.

MIN 1-0 TOR

ALCS Game 2

October 9, 1991

The Blue Jays even the series with a 5-2 win in the Metrodome. Toronto's Joe Carter and Kelly Gruber get 2 RBIs each securing the win for pitcher Juan Guzman.

MIN 1-1 TOR

ALCS Game 3

October 11, 1991

The Blue Jays score 2 runs in the first inning against Scott Erickson, but that would be it. The Twins force extra innings and score in the 10th setting up Rick Aguilera to save the first game in Toronto putting the Twins back in front in the series.

MIN 2-1 TOR

ALCS Game 4

October 12, 1991

The Twins dominate game 4 with multi hit games from 4 hitters. Dan Gladden and Kirby Puckett stand out as the Twins offense scores 9 runs which is plenty against the two allowed from Jack Morris and Steve Bedrosian. This puts Toronto in an elimination game infront of their home fans.

MIN 3-1 TOR

ALCS Game 5

October 13, 1991

In a series clinching win, the Twins rally back from down 5-2 after the 5th inning. 3 runs scored in the 6th and 8th innings gives them a 8-5 lead which would never be relenquised. Rick Aguilera saves his third game of the series as the Twins go back to the World Series.

MIN 4-1 TOR

World Series Game 1

October 19, 1991

The Twins win the opening game of the World Series at home 5-2. Kent Hrbek and Greg Gagne hit home runs late to put the game away. Chuck Knoblauch fills the box score going 3 for 3 with a walk and a stolen base.

MIN 1-0 ATL

World Series Game 2

October 20, 1991

The Twins win their 5th straight game to take a 2-0 series lead against the Braves. Kevin Tapani bests Hall of Famer Tom Glavine in a 3-2 pitcher's duel as both starters throw for 8 innings. Poor defense kills the Braves as only 1 of Glavine's 3 runs is earned. Rick Aguilera earns his 5th save of the playoffs.

MIN 2-0 ATL

World Series Game 3

October 22, 1991

The Braves win a much needed game 3 in a 12 inning marathon. The Twins score 2 runs in the 8th to tie it, but Rick Aguilera lets in the winning run as the 13th pitcher of the game.

MIN 2-1 ATL

World Series Game 4

October 23, 1991

The Braves even the series with another walk off victory. John Smoltz and Jack Morris pitch excellently allowed just 3 runs. The final score is 3-2 making it the third straight game decided by a single run.

MIN 2-2 ATL

World Series Game 5

October 24, 1991

The last game played in Atlanta is all Braves. After going down 5-0, the Twins cut the lead to 2 with a 3 run 6th inning. The Braves score 6 in response in the 7th. The Braves collected 17 hits in this game including 3 from Ron Gant and Greg Olson. The Braves win 14-5 taking the series lead.

MIN 2-3 ATL

World Series Game 6

October 26, 1991

The Twins force game 7 with a legendary performance from Kirby Puckett. Back in Minnesota, Puckett makes an incredible catch at the wall in the third inning and eventually hits the walk off home run in the bottom of the 11th.

MIN 3-3 ATL

World Series Game 7

October 27, 1991

The World Series ends with an instant classic drama of a game. This game is the fourth decided by one run and the fourth walk off of the series. Neither team scores through the first nine and a half innings thanks to remarkable pitching performances from John Smoltz and Jack Morris. In the bottom of the 10th, Dan Gladden scores on a Gene Larkin single to walk off the World Series. This gives Jack Morris his 4th win of the postseason earning the Minnesota native the MVP of the World Series

MIN 4-3 ATL
